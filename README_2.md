# Giga Tech

**Renewable energy & engineering** — Chinhoyi & Harare, Zimbabwe
🌐 [gigatechzw.com](https://gigatechzw.com)

Giga Tech is a registered Zimbabwean engineering company delivering solar
power systems and electrical services, built on a mechatronics engineering
foundation. Where most installers sell panels, we design, install, and
maintain engineered energy systems — and we're building toward energy
intelligence and industrial automation.

---

## What We Do

**Solar Installation**
Residential and commercial solar systems, from design and supply through
installation and maintenance. Commercial offerings from 10kVA to 60kVA.

**Electrical Services**
Professional electrical work delivered to certifiable standard, backed by
qualified engineering rather than guesswork.

**Where We're Headed**
Smart metering, remote system monitoring, and — long term — industrial
automation, SCADA, and IoT for Zimbabwe's industry and agriculture. See the
full phased plan in [ROADMAP.md](./ROADMAP.md).

---

## Why Giga Tech

- **Engineering-led.** Founded by mechatronics engineers (Chinhoyi University of Technology). Every system is designed, not assembled.
- **Built for this market.** Sized, priced, and specified for Zimbabwean homes, farms, and businesses — load shedding is the problem we solve.
- **Accountable.** Registered company, traceable workmanship, and a growing portfolio of completed installations.
