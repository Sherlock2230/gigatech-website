About Us

Who Is Giga Tech? Engineered Power for Zimbabwean Homes and Businesses

Giga Tech · July 2026 · Chinhoyi & Harare, Zimbabwe

If you live or run a business in Zimbabwe, you already know the problem we exist to solve. Load shedding does not ask whether you have a fridge full of stock, a borehole that needs to pump, or a family trying to study after dark. Power cuts cost money, comfort and opportunity — every single day.

Giga Tech is a registered Zimbabwean engineering company that designs, installs and maintains solar power systems and electrical installations, serving Chinhoyi, Harare and the surrounding regions. We were founded by mechatronics engineers, and that word — engineers — is the difference we want you to remember.

Panels Are Easy to Buy. Systems Have to Be Engineered.

Anyone can sell you a panel and an inverter. What determines whether your solar investment actually works — through winter, through cloudy weeks, five years from now — is the engineering behind it: sizing the system to your real loads, matching the battery bank to your usage pattern, protecting the installation properly, and wiring it to a standard that keeps your family and property safe.

We don't sell panels. We design power systems around the way you actually live and work.

Every Giga Tech installation starts with an assessment of your actual energy needs, not a guess. We then design a system on paper before a single screw goes into your roof, install it to certifiable standard, and stand behind it with a written warranty. You can read exactly how a project runs from first call to handover on our How We Work page.

What We Do

Residential solar. Backup and full solar systems for homes — sized so the things that matter to you keep running when ZESA goes down, without paying for capacity you'll never use.

Commercial solar. Systems from 10kVA to 60kVA for shops, offices, farms and industry, designed around the numbers: your loads, your tariff, your payback period.

Electrical services. Professional electrical work delivered by qualified engineers — done right the first time, because in this trade shortcuts are dangerous and expensive.

Where We're Going

Solar and electrical work is our foundation, not our ceiling. Giga Tech is building toward smart energy monitoring — so you can see what your system is producing and using from your phone — and, in the long run, industrial automation for Zimbabwe's factories, mines and farms. When you install with us, you're joining a company that plans to be servicing and upgrading your system a decade from now, not a briefcase operation that disappears after handover.
