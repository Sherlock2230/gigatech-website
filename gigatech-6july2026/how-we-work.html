How we work

One team.
Start to switch-on.

Most bad solar experiences come from too many hands: one person sells it, another supplies it, a third installs it — and nobody owns the result. We do it differently. One engineering team carries your project from first measurement to final handshake.

The full process

EPC, explained in plain English.

EPC stands for Engineering, Procurement and Construction. It's how serious energy projects are delivered worldwide — and it's how we deliver yours, whether it's a 3kVA home kit or a 60kVA commercial plant.

1
Engineering · Week 1

Free site assessment

We visit your property, check your roof and wiring, and measure what you actually use. No guessing — we look at your real appliances, your real habits and your real bills.

2
Engineering · Week 1–2

Your system, on paper first

Our engineers design a system sized precisely for your needs and budget. You receive a clear proposal: what goes in, what it powers, what it costs, and how long it takes. One fixed price, in writing.

3
Procurement · Week 2–3

We source the equipment

We buy panels, inverters and batteries only from brands we've tested on real Zimbabwean installations. You benefit from our trade pricing and genuine manufacturer warranties — no grey imports, no fakes.

4
Construction · Week 3–4

Professional installation

Our own team installs everything — neat cabling, proper earthing, safe switchgear. We test every circuit and simulate a power cut before we call it done, so the first real outage is a non-event.

5
Handover & beyond

Walkthrough, warranty, support

We show you exactly how your system works, hand over all documentation and warranties, and stay one phone call away. Maintenance plans are available if you'd rather never think about it again.

The engineering behind us

Why the maths matters.

You don't need to understand the engineering — that's our job. But here's what our qualified engineers quietly get right on every project, and why it matters to you.

Right-sizing

We calculate your true daily usage before quoting. That's the difference between a system that struggles by year two and one that still performs in year twenty.

Battery life protection

Batteries are the most expensive part of your system. We design so yours charge and discharge gently — which can double how long they last.

Safety by design

Correct cable sizes, proper earthing, lightning protection and safe changeover switching — the invisible work that protects your family, your staff and your property.

Built for Zimbabwean conditions

Long outages, voltage swings and hot roofs are our normal, not an edge case. Every design assumes the grid will misbehave — because it will.

Degree-qualified team

Giga Tech was founded by university-trained engineers. Your system is calculated by people who studied this — not improvised on your roof.

Tested before handover

We don't leave until we've simulated a real power cut and watched your system carry the load. You see it work before you pay the final invoice.
